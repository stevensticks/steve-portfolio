Author

This is the start of a new idea for Needle & Rotation. I’ve been spending a lot of time looking into the past as far as music goes lately and I have been enjoying it immensely. I’ve listened to more records from the 60s in the month of January then I did for the whole of 2014.

That being said, I have been listening to other things as well though. I try to stay current with what is being released every week, as well as listening to other records that I love. So the idea here is to list some of the other things that has been happening in my music world in the past week and a few words about how I’ve been feeling about them currently. (Sometime I don’t like things for the first few listens and it grows on me. So records can repeat themselves on this list week to week as I grow more fond of them.) I also want to include shows that I’ve been to in the past week and whatever else this column may grow in to.
