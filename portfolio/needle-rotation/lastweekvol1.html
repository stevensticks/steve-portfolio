Last Week In Music

Volume #1

Kanye West - I was so excited on New Years Day to read that Kanye had released a new song. This excitement was quickly shattered after I hit play. It was the first time I’ve been truly disappointed in something that he has done. A couple weeks later he released another track. An even bigger disappointment.

Panda Bear - Panda Bear vs. Grim Reaper - This was the first record I got behind this year. I really haven’t spent too much time with him previously but am looking forward to digging deeper.

Viet Cong - Viet Cong - I had high hopes for this one. It didn’t disappoint. Has everything I’m looking for in a great Post-Rock album.

Cymbals Eat Guitars - Lose - This one of last years finer releases and it’s still been getting a lot of plays recently. It’s one of those Rock records that seems simple on the surface but the more you listen the more it opens itself up.

Lupe Fiasco - Tetsuo & Youth - This is a record that has yet to grab me yet I keep coming back to it every once in a while because maybe I’m missing something. Lupe has the potential to be a great rapper but it seems, especially lately, he’s been missing the mark.

Twin Shadow - There are 3 songs that you can listen to now from his up coming record. To The Top, Turn Me Up & Old Love / New Love. All three are fantastic. One of my most anticipated releases of the year.

Marty Robbins - Gunfighter Ballads & Trail Songs - This is an album I stumbled upon through a random Instagram profile. A classic from ’59 that I’d never heard. After hearing 3 songs I was blown away and ordered it on eBay. Great songs and great lyrics.

Sigur Ros & Fever Ray - There is something about the winter that alway brings me to these artists.

Kendrick Lamar - YES YES YES

P.S. The Grammys are a fucking joke and don’t matter. I didn’t watch them.
