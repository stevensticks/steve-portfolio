Shoot The Chorus 2 - No Hook

My dear friend Graeme’s a wonderful musician. You should listen to him. You can check him out here www.graemekennedymusic.com. On his website he has a couple of mixtapes. One is titled “Shoot the Chorus.” It’s also deserving of a good listen. It features songs that lack or at least take the long way to the chorus.
